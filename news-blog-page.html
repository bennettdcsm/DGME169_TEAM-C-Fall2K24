CSM Digital Media Students Shine at Local Film Festival

San Mateo, CA – The College of San Mateo's Digital Media program is proud to announce the success of its students, Annie Lee, at the recent San Francisco International Film Festival. Several student-produced films were selected for screening, and one film, "Pixel Dreams," won the award for Best Student Short Film.
